About Me

I'm currently a Games Programming student at Staffordshire University, learning how to effectively program games and their accompanying systems. I grew up in Stoke-On-Trent, England, where I developed a love for games and how they work. Even moreso, I love to see ambition in games, that being features and ideas that've never really been done before, or those difficult enough to dissuade the majority from making an attempt. I've been lucky enough to see this firsthand during my time in industry. If you'd like to get in touch (and maybe talk a bit about AI philosophy whilst we're at it) please see my links in the bar on the left. On a sidenote, I sometimes do photography.
